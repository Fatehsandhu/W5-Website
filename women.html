Investing in Women’s Futures

What We Do

Through the Investing in Women's Futures (IWF) program, we deliver flexible programs and services to address the needs of immigrant and newcomer women in the Windsor-Essex community. The program priorities activities that promote:

1. Prevention of violence against women
2. Economic security for women

Program Offers:

Through the Investing in Women's Futures (IWF), we deliver creative and flexible programs and services to address the immigrant and newcomer women in the Windsor-Essex community.

Violence Against Women Counselling & Support - counselling, worshops, information sessions

Navigating Systems Support - assistance with matters pertaining to law, housing, financial supports, housing, health, etc

Pre-Employment Training - Identify career goals, create job plan, prepare resume, develop interview skills

Computer Skills Development Training - Microsost Word & Excel, Internet, Email, Social Media, Typing

Life Skills Training - knitting, crochet, English communication circles
